Public Awareness Warning

O!Save warns the public against fake online recruitment activities. Unauthorized individuals and entities are using O!Save's name and trademark to deceive job seekers. O!Save exclusively publishes employment opportunities through verified job platforms. We do not utilize public email domains such as Gmail, Yahoo, Mail, or similar platforms for recruitment correspondence. Any social media pages or website bearing resemblance to O!Save's official branding are fraudulent. Please exercise caution with unsolicited job posting claiming to represent O!Save, particularly those communicated through social media or unofficial channels.
