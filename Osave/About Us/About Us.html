About O!SAVE

O!Save Trading Philippines Corporation is a hard discount supermarket chain founded in 2021. Established in 2021. Backed by Robinsons Retail Holdings, it launched its first branch in January 2022 in Pampanga and expanded to over 750 locations by late 2025.

The O!SAVE Philosophy
  • We Carefully Select Each Of Our Products

    • We focus on basic food, household and personal care products which are necessary for your everyday life. We pick the best quality and choices for our customers.

  • We Offer The Lowest Possible Price Every Day

    • Our stores are simple and not larger than needed. We do not rent stores in expensive locations and avoid unnecessary expenses. Instead of promotions, we offer consistently low prices every day.

  • Our Guarantee

    • We provide an unconditional return guarantee. If you are not satisfied with any product, simply return it to any O!SAVE store for a full refund.
